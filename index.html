3
PREDICTION ERROR

4
FREE ENERGY SURGE

不確定性累積，大腦發出警報
Uncertainty accumulates; brain triggers alarm
5
RESPONSE CHOICE

Avoid (amplifies) or explore (reduces)
6
MODEL UPDATE
更新預測模型
新資訊整合 → 誤差降低 → 焦慮下降
New info integrated → errors reduce → anxiety drops
